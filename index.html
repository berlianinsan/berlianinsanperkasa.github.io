About Us

Established in 2008 with more than 10 years of experience in design and construction, our professional team work closely with the engineers, architects as well as contractor and owners. Our completed project stand as proof of the quality of our work, the fact that most of our client retain enduring relationship with us as further proof that not only is our work excellent but also that our concern for our client is paramount importance. We look forward to lending our expertise and experiences to new clients as well as established clients with new project in mind. We uniquely suited to meet the demands of even the most intricate custom design. Not only do we offer quality product at competitive price, delivery to your site will be on time and we promise to deliver outstanding products anytime. It is our mission to be as efficient and productive as possible.

Our Services

As a full-service construction company, PT. Berlian Insan Perkasa essentially assists clients from the very out set to the completion of the project. We become partners with our clients in understanding the five basic functions:

  • Defining and establishing the scope of the construction project.
  • Pre-construction project planning including preparation and scheduling the project, phasing plan. budgeting/cost-modelling, subcontractor qualification, etc.
  • On-site execution of Ihe project includes scheduling, quality control, safe management. status reporting and change management.
  • Monitoring and controlling projects.
  • Closing the project related to the post construction activities.
